Frequently Asked Questions

Is VeloServe itself free?

Yes. VeloServe is open-source under the MIT license. The paid plans cover platform features like license management, priority support, pre-built binaries, and monitoring.

Can I switch plans later?

Absolutely. Upgrade or downgrade at any time from your platform dashboard. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle.

What does migration assistance include?

Our team helps you migrate from nginx/Apache + PHP-FPM to VeloServe SAPI. We review your configuration, test your applications, and ensure zero-downtime deployment.

Do I need a license key to use VeloServe?

No. You can build from source and run VeloServe without any license. License keys unlock platform features like heartbeat monitoring and support tickets.
